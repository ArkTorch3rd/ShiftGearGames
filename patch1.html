Infinite Blades Patch notes 1.0.1

The Eden Patch

The patch has been denoted as the "Eden Patch" because of Eden's bugged hitboxes and oversights.

Here are the following issues that have been identified.

  1. Down special: Royal Decree has a wider hitbox than intended
  2. 2C Heavy: Ejection's hitbox occasionally never registers.
  3. Hitbox for Finisher: Sanctuary has map-wide coverage.
  4. Neutral Special: Purifying Fields covers more ground area than intended.
All but Purifying Fields have been fixed and Purifying Fields has been kept at its current radius.

We will be observing the performance of every fighter to make more balance changes.
