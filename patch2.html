Infinite Blades Patch notes 1.0.2

Wild Patch

The patch has been denoted as the "Wild Patch" because of certain characters being too dominant in the current meta.

Here are the following balance changes made.

  • Atlas:
    1. Damage of Up special: Fang-Upper has been reduced by 10%
    2. More clarity is added to Neutral B: Fang-Projectile
  • X-ros
    1. Rotary Cannon Neutral B: Thermal Cannon's damage reduced by 15%

We will be observing the performance of every fighter to make more balance changes.
