Shadowblade

A visual novel series that follows a tale of a vigilante in a fantasy world. Play as the vigilante Atlas and mete out your brand of justice in this choose-your-own-adventure visual novel.

Immerse yourself as Atlas, learning how he functions as a person and a vigilante. Observe the world around you and prepare for any mysterious circumstance. And most importantly, Survive.
